Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 18, 2026. Operated by Deesca, an independent developer ("we", "us"). Contact: deescaapp@gmail.com.

Who we are

Deesca is a mobile app that helps couples move from conflict to clarity using AI-assisted mediation, coaching, and reflection.

Data we collect

  • Account: your email address (passwordless sign-in) and, if you choose, Sign in with Apple identifiers.
  • You provide: your name and your partner's name/details, relationship status, onboarding answers, the text of arguments and reflections you enter, screenshots you upload, and voice recordings you make.
  • Generated: AI analyses (mediation reports, cooldown reports, red-flag analyses), a per-user "relationship memory" summary, mood/journal entries, and usage counts.
  • Automatic: basic device/diagnostic data and, if enabled, crash logs.
  • Purchases: subscription status via Apple/RevenueCat. We never receive your full payment-card details.

How we use it

To provide and personalize the app, generate AI guidance, remember context across sessions, enforce fair-use limits, process subscriptions, and maintain security. We do not sell your data or use it for advertising.

Audio

Voice recordings are sent for transcription, then discarded — we do not retain the raw audio. The resulting text is stored as your session/journal content.

AI & sub-processors

Your inputs are processed by third-party providers to deliver features:

  • Supabase — database, authentication, storage, hosting.
  • Anthropic (Claude) — text/vision reasoning.
  • OpenAI — audio transcription.
  • RevenueCat + Apple — subscriptions.

These providers process data under their own terms and do not use your content to train their models for this use.

Storage, security, retention

Data is stored on Supabase with row-level security so each user can access only their own rows. We keep your data while your account is active and delete it on account deletion.

Your rights & choices

  • Access/export your data: contact us.
  • Delete everything: Settings → Delete account permanently erases your account and all associated data (sessions, journals, coach memory, partner details).
  • Manage subscription: through your Apple ID settings.

Children

Deesca is not directed to anyone under 17 (or the age of digital consent in your region).

Changes

We may update this policy and will post the new date above.
