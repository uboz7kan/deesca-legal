Common questions

  • Manage or cancel your subscription: open the app's Settings → Manage subscription, or go to iPhone Settings → your name → Subscriptions.
  • Restore a purchase: Settings → Restore purchases (use the same Apple ID you subscribed with).
  • Delete your account and data: Settings → Delete account permanently erases your account and all associated data (sessions, journals, coach memory, partner details).
  • Trouble signing in: request a fresh magic-link email and check your spam folder. Still stuck? Email us.
